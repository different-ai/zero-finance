This week on weloveyourstartup we're featuring a recent raise by Lava.

Lava provides the world's first bitcoin-backed line of credit, enabling holders to access dollar liquidity in under a second without selling their BTC. With just 5% interest (+ 2% capital charge), true self-custody through Discreet Log Contracts, and global availability from $100 to $1B+, they're making it finally possible to use Bitcoin as collateral without rehypothecation risk.

We love to see DeFi maturing and moving toward self-custodial, institutional-grade infrastructure that doesn't force users to choose between holding their assets and accessing liquidity.

Congrats to @lava_xyz on the $200M Series B.
